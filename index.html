What is AI Governance?

AI Governance is the framework of policies, processes, and practices that ensure AI systems are developed, deployed, and managed responsibly, ethically, and in compliance with regulations.

Risk Management

Identify, assess, and mitigate risks associated with AI systems throughout their lifecycle.

Ethics & Compliance

Ensure AI systems align with ethical principles and regulatory requirements.

Transparency

Promote explainability and accountability in AI decision-making processes.

Core Principles

Foundational principles for responsible AI development and deployment

Fairness & Non-Discrimination

Ensure AI systems treat all individuals and groups fairly, without bias or discrimination.

Transparency & Explainability

Make AI decision-making processes understandable and explainable to relevant stakeholders.

Privacy & Security

Protect personal data and ensure robust security measures throughout the AI lifecycle.

Accountability & Human Oversight

Establish clear accountability mechanisms and maintain meaningful human control over AI systems.

Robustness & Safety

Ensure AI systems are reliable, secure, and function safely in their intended environments.

Sustainability & Environmental Impact

Consider the environmental impact of AI systems and promote sustainable practices.
